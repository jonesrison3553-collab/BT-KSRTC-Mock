Trip Itinerary

5:00 AM
Departure — Pathanamthitta Depot
KSRTC bus departs. Comfortable seating. Bring snacks for the ride.
8:30 AM
Reach Gavi Forest Gate
Forest entry check. Permits handled by trip coordinator.
9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Forest Walk & Wildlife Spotting
Guided walk along the Gavi reservoir. Elephant & Gaur sightings common in morning hours.
1:00 PM
Lunch Break
Packed lunch provided OR stop at the forest canteen. Vegetarian only inside forest.
2:00 – 5:00 PM
Gavi Dam Viewpoint & Boating
Optional boating (₹100 extra, pay on the spot). Scenic walk around the dam.
5:30 PM
Return Journey
Bus departs for Pathanamthitta.
~8:00 PM
Reach Pathanamthitta Depot
Trip ends. Travel time may vary based on road conditions.

  • ❌ Not ideal for luxury seekers
